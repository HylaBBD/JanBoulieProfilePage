My school

I went to school for the first time with my dad when I was just 10 weeks old! How smart am I? My dad took me every Saturday until I graduated puppy school at only 3 months old!

After puppy school I went to beginners class and flew through the lessons! After four months, my teacher told me I could go straight to advanced class with the big dogs! I am now "Advanced-class Boulie" :).
